F2E A food sharing application

About

Our mission is to reduce food waste through social sharing. At F2E we strive to reduce food waste in Vancouver. This can be achieved by integrating social media to trade with friends and family. F2E bridges this gap by offering a platform to share, post, and request food or ingredients that may be thrown out. We make sure that all items are safe and of fine quality through a series of identification and validation. We are confident that F2E will bring a conscious effort to reduce food waste around Vancouver.

Sharing

F2E makes sharing food and ingredients with your friends easy. Users are able to post, request, and search for food to share.

Vancouver Food Waste

Food waste is a major problem in Vancouver. F2E is an effort to combat food waste by redistributing food and ingredients. We hope that sharing in the community helps increase awareness. Love Food Hate Waste specializes in informing Metro Vancouver about food waste practices, check them out!
